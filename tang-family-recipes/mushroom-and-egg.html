Mushroom & Egg

Probably not an original dish, but honestly I haven't eaten this anywhere else. Simple and delicious.

Ingredients

NOTE: Amounts unspecified, but a good rule of thumb is that the mushrooms and eggs should be approximately equal when mixed together, otherwise the egg will overwhelm the mushrooms, or the mixture will fall apart when stir-fried.

  • baby bella mushrooms
  • eggs
  • salt
  • vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Wash and dice the mushrooms.
  2. Crack the eggs in a large mixing bowl. Mix.
  3. Throw in the mushrooms and mix.
  4. Sprinkle in salt to flavor the mixture.
  5. Heat a wok with oil on medium-high.
  6. Pour in and stir-fry the mixture.
  7. When the egg starts browning, plate the dish. Enjoy!
