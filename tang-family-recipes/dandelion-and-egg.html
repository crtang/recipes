Dandelion Leaves & Eggs

(Yes, that weed in your front yard is edible.)

An herby and slightly salty dish, this plate is great for canker sores.

Ingredients

NOTE: Amounts unspecified, but a good rule of thumb is that the leaves and egg should be approximately equal when mixed together, otherwise the egg will overwhelm the leaves, or the mixture will fall apart when stir-fried.

  • dandelion leaves
  • eggs
  • salt
  • [OPTIONAL] black pepper
  • vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Wash and dice the dandelion leaves.
  2. Crack the eggs into a large mixing bowl. Mix them thoroughly.
  3. Throw the dandelion leaves into the egg and mix again.
  4. Sprinkle enough salt to flavor the egg and dandelion mix. If you want black pepper, this is the time to add it.
  5. Heat up a wok or pan with oil on medium.
  6. Pour the mix into the wok and stir-fry.
  7. When eggs have started browning and an aroma is wafting out, plate the dish. Enjoy!
